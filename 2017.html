    Here are the links to each of the challenges we did.
    Challenge 0. Driving Challenge: Navigate an obstacle course in the shortest time using the fewest keystrokes.
    Challenge 1. Dance Challenge: Make your robot perform a “dance” in response to a single keystroke. The dance must include a sequence of at least 4 movements, including driving forward, reversing and rotating.
